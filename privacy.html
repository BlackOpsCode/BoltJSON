Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026

BoltJSON processes all files locally in your browser.
Your JSON data is never uploaded to any server.
We do not collect, store, or sell personal data.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how RuntimeZero ("we", "us") handles information when you use BoltJSON, accessible at runtime-hub.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring full transparency.

2. Information we do not collect

BoltJSON is designed with privacy as a core principle. Specifically:

  • We do not receive, process, or store any files you open in BoltJSON.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not use advertising trackers, third-party analytics SDKs, or session-replay tools.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Local processing

All file parsing is performed entirely within your browser using JavaScript. Files are loaded into browser memory only, processed locally, and displayed on screen. When you close the tab or refresh, all data is discarded automatically.

4. Cookies and storage

BoltJSON does not set any first-party cookies. We do not use localStorage, IndexedDB, or any persistent browser storage to save your files or session data.

5. Analytics

We may collect basic, anonymised, aggregated analytics such as page views and browser type through privacy-respecting tools that do not track individual users and do not use cookies. This data cannot be used to identify you.

6. Third-party services

BoltJSON loads fonts from Google Fonts over HTTPS. This may transmit your IP address to Google as part of the CDN request. Refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

7. Children's privacy

BoltJSON is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance.
